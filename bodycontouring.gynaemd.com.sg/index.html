Services Provided

Our clinic offers the MAXIMUS TRILIPO RADIOFREQUENCY (RF) & TRILIPO DYNAMIC MUSCLE ACTIVATION (DMA) TREATMENT, which combines triple action for Facial & Body Contouring, Skin Tightening, Fat Reduction.

With the Maximus TriLipo technology, surgery-averse patients can contour their face & body, reduce fat & cellulite and tighten their skin without undergoing invasive surgical procedures. TriLipo targets and removes unwanted localized fat deposits.

The Maximus system, powered by TriLipo technology uses a combination of focused TriLipo Radio Frequency (RF) technology and TriLipo Dynamic Muscle Activation (DMA) to achieve non-invasive body shaping. Maximus TriLipo RF technology treatment provides a holistic solution for a variety of aesthetic applications like

  • Fat reduction

  • Skin tightening

What To Expect On Your Initial Visit for a Body Contouring Consultation

The success and effectiveness of your body contouring treatment depends not solely on the procedure itself but also very much on your ability to lead a healthy lifestyle and diet. You’ll be asked a number of questions about your health, desires and lifestyle.

Be prepared to discuss:

  • Why you want the procedure, your realistic expectations and desired outcome
  • The treatment options available for body contouring
  • Your medical conditions, drug allergies
  • Previous surgeries

Your doctor may also:

  • Evaluate your general health status and any pre-existing health conditions or risk factors
  • Examine and measure your body, including detailed measurements
  • Take before and after photographs for your medical record
  • Discuss your options and recommend a course of treatment
  • Discuss likely outcomes of your procedure and any risks or potential complications

Meeting with the Aesthetic Doctor

A detailed informed consent and having realistic expectations will result in better treatment satisfaction. If you have any concern or question about the procedure, please feel free to share that with your doctor.

FAQ

  • Am I a candidate for this non-surgical procedure?

    Treatment with the non-surgical Maximus TriLipo technology is designed to be a clinic-based treatment for body contouring and fat reduction so patients can return to their daily routine with minimal disruption and down-time. It is recommended for both men and women who desire a more firmed and toned appearance. It is also designed to help post-natal women regain their figure after delivery.

  • Does bruising or swelling result from Maximus Trilipo?

    The Maximus TriLipo technology is safe and should not cause bruising or swelling.

  • What other aesthetic conditions can be treated with this machine?

    The Maximus TriLipo and TriFractional RF procedure can treat a range of aesthetic conditions such as wrinkle reduction, acne scars, facial contouring, skin tightening, cellulite reduction, circumferential reduction, stretch marks as well as lifting, toning and firming of muscles.

  • How much are the charges?

    Depending on the different body areas which require treatment, your doctor will recommend the number of sessions needed and advise on the treatment cost accordingly.

  • Can I walk in for an appointment or service?

    We value your time so we request that you make an appointment with our staff so that you can be seen on time. A time slot will then be allocated for our doctor to see you. Please call or email us for an appointment.
