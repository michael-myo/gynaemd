About Dr Christopher Ng

Dr Christopher Ng was educated in Singapore and the United Kingdom. His early desire to pursue a career as an Obstetrician and Gynaecologist stemmed from having two generations of prominent doctors in his family. Dr Ng complements his practice in obstetrics and gynaecology with aesthetics. He is an advocate of clinically proven aesthetic treatments with minimal downtime.

He has mentored and attended courses conducted by aesthetic medicine physicians including Dr Melvin Elson, Dr Michel Delune, Dr Pierre Fournier, Dr Patrick Micheels, Dr Gustavo Leibaschoff‎, Dr Byung-Soon Park, Dr Sooil Chun, Dr Kyung Tae Hong and Prof Goh Chee Leok from USA, Belgium, France, Switzerland, Argentina, South Korea and Singapore to further refine his knowledge and techniques.

His passion for aesthetics has brought him to conferences/courses in Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, South Korea and France. He has completed aesthetic courses run by the American Academy of Aesthetic Medicine and by the Anteis InjectNow Academy™ at the Mérieux Foundation in Annecy, France. He has also obtained the Certificates of Competence in all aesthetic procedures recognised by the Singapore Medical Council Aesthetic Practice Oversight Committee.

To enhance his obstetric, gynaecology and aesthetic practice, Dr Ng is a firm believer in good nutrition, health supplements in addition to conventional medication to optimize the body’s natural capacity to heal from within. He has attended courses by Prof Ian Brighthope in Australia and obtained his certificate of achievement in nutrition and herbal protocols in 2002.

FAQ

  • What aesthetic treatment machines does your clinic offer?

    Spectra™ Laser

    It is a non-ablative skin rejuvenation treatment. This two-step thermal rejuvenation process is aimed at the treatment of enlarged pores, acne scars, fine wrinkle reduction and melasma. With minimal downtime and reduced treatment intervals, patients can return to their normal lives shortly after treatment. Spectra™ Laser is designed for a range of indications and skin types.

    Maximus TriLipo RF

    With the Maximus TriLipo technology, surgery-averse patients can contour their face & body, reduce fat & cellulite and tighten their skin without undergoing invasive surgical procedures. It targets and eliminates localized fat deposits which reduces cellulite appearance, contours your body and decreases the circumference of the abdomen, arms, flanks and thighs. TriLipo augments collagen regeneration and yields skin tightening to treat wrinkles on the face, neck, chin and cheeks.

    NOVAPlus 3R IPL

    The NOVAPlus 3R IPL uses Tunable Broadband Emission Technology to Remodel, Rejuvenate and Renew. It can be used to treat stretch marks, acne and acne scars, enlarged pores, uneven skin tone, rosacea, fine spider veins, broken capillaries, pigment lesions, sagging skin, fine wrinkles and hair removal.

  • How much improvement in my skin can I expect after the treatment?

    This depends on your presenting condition, the treatment procedure and the care you take after your treatment. We will show you before and after pictures so that you will have a realistic idea of what can be achieved but improvement varies from person to person and also depends on the individual’s ability to heal in the end.

  • What are the charges?

    First consultation charges start from $150 and depending on the type of aesthetic treatment, your doctor will explain the cost involved.

  • Can I walk in for an appointment or service?

    Yes you can but you may then have to wait. In order to be seen on time, you are strongly advised to make an appointment with our staff to ensure that a time slot is allocated for our doctor to see you. Please call or email us for an appointment.
